Clubs & Activities

Future Students

We organize orientation programs to welcome new students to our community, introduce them to campus resources and support services, connect them with faculty and staff, and help them build relationships with their peers.

Current Students

Many of our students exhibit leadership potential and take initiative in both academic and extracurricular pursuits, assuming roles of responsibility and guiding their peers toward common goals

Alumni students

At our school, we aim to prepare students for a wide range of fields and careers, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and experiences needed to succeed in today's dynamic job market. Here are some of the fields and careers that our students often venture into after graduating

  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science and Analytics
  • Computer Programming and Development
  • Biotechnology and Pharmaceutical Research
  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Environmental Science and Engineering

Music

Our students have access to a variety of musical instruments, including keyboards, guitars, drums, and more, allowing them to explore their musical interests and talents. Classes are conducted in our well-equipped music rooms, complete with audiovisual resources and technology to enhance learning.

Writing

At our school, the Writing Club provides a vibrant space for students to explore their passion for writing and storytelling. Led by dedicated faculty advisors, the club offers a supportive community where students of all backgrounds and skill levels can come together to share ideas, hone their writing skills, and unleash their creativity.

Dance

The Dance Club at our school is a vibrant and inclusive community where students come together to express themselves through movement and rhythm. Led by passionate instructors and student leaders, the club offers a diverse range of dance styles, from contemporary and hip-hop to ballet, jazz, and cultural dances from around the world.
